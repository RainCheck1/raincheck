FAQ

What is Rainline?

Rainline is a simple wager on rainfall for your event. If the event meets your selected rain threshold, your claim pays out.

How much can I stake?

Base stake is 10% of your ticket total*. Only one Rainline stake can be added per checkout.

When do I get paid?

Stakes are saved as pending at checkout. Settlement happens after the event finishes. In the demo claim flow, you can choose a Win or Lose outcome immediately.

How do I start?

Start by selecting an event in Explore. Checkout is locked until an event is chosen.
