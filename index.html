FAQ

What is Rainline?

Rainline is a simple wager on rainfall for your event. If the event meets your selected rain threshold, your claim pays out based on your stake and odds — capped at your ticket price.

How much can I stake?

Minimum stake is $10. Maximum stake is limited so that your win payout can’t exceed your ticket price. Only one Rainline stake can be added per checkout.

When do I get paid?

Stakes are saved as pending at checkout. Settlement happens after the event finishes. In the demo claim flow, you can choose a Win or Lose outcome immediately.

Do I need to start from checkout?

No — start by selecting an event in Explore events. Checkout is locked until an event is chosen.
